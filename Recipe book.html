New York Cheese Cake recipe

  • Time: 1 1/4 hours
  • Serves: 6-8
Ingredients Quantity
Cookie crumbs A bowl
Melted butter 1/2
Full fat cream cheese Three
Caster sugar One full small bowl
Vanilla extract One full small cup
Finely grated lemon rind Half of the lemon peel
Lemon juice 1/3 spoon
Plain flour A full small bowl
Eggs Four
Full fat sour cream One full small bowl

Step 1: Prepare the cookie crumb base. Mix melted butter and cookie crumbs together until moist and evenly coated.
Step 2: Press the mixture very firmly into the base and sides of a greased and lined 23cm springform pan. Chill in the fridge for at least 30 minutes.
Step 3: Prepare the filling. Beat the cream cheese, sugar, vanilla, lemon juice and lemon rind with electric beaters until just combined.
Step 4: Stir through the sour cream and pour into the prepared tin. You can fill the mixture almost to the very top as the cheesecake will not rise.
Step 5: Bake low and slow and place into a low oven set to 140 degrees celsius (fan-forced) or 160 degrees celsius (conventional).
Step 6: Bake for 1 1/4 hours.
Step 7: Turn off the oven and leave the cheesecake inside. Leave in the oven with the door closed for 2 hours.
Step 8: After two hours, remove the cheesecake from the oven and place onto the bench to cool completely.
Step 9: Chill in the refrigerator. Place the cheesecake (still in the baking tin) into the fridge for a minimum of 4 hours (preferably overnight).
Step 10: Allow to come to room temperature before serving. Remove the cheesecake from the fridge 1 hour before serving and decorate as you wish.

Source: Classic New York Baked Cheese Cake

Chocolate chip cookies recipe

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 10
Ingredients Quantity
Softened butter 150g
Brown sugar 1/2 cup
Caster sugar 1/2 cup
Egg 1
Vanilla extract 1 teaspoon
Plain flour 1 3/4 cups
Milk chocolate bits 1/2 cup + 1 tablespoon extra
Dark chcocolate bits 1/2 cup + 1 tablespoon extra

Step 1: Preheat oven to 180C or 160C fan-forced. Line 2 baking trays with baking paper. Using an electric mixer or whisking by hand, beat butter and sugars and 1-2 minutes or until smooth and well combined. Beat in egg and vanilla until combined.
Step 2: Stir in flour. Stir in dark and milk choc bits. Roll 2 level tablespoonfuls of mixture into balls and place on prepared trays, 3cm apart. Press down slightly. Decorate with extra choc bits.
Step 3: Bake for 15-18 minutes or until light golden and cooked. Transfer to a wire rack to cool. Store in an airtight container for up to 1 week.

Source: Chocolate chip cookies

Vanilla Milkshake recipe

  • Time: 5 minutes
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Vanilla ice cream 5 scoop
Milk 1 cup
Vanilla extract 1 teaspoon

Step 1: In blender, combine ice cream, milk, and vanilla, and blend until mixture is smooth and frothy. Pour into 2 tall glasses.
